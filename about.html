My Story

From Storyteller to Strategic Partner

I'm Jessica Marabella — a communications and marketing consultant with deep experience across publishing, nonprofit, and professional services. I help organizations find the words, and the strategy, to move people.

My career has been built at the intersection of craft and strategy. I've spent years developing messaging frameworks, running PR campaigns, building content engines, and helping leaders communicate with authenticity. The through-line in all of it: clear thinking leads to clear communication, and clear communication drives results.

I'm drawn to organizations at moments of growth or transition — when there's something important to say and the stakes are high. I work best when there's a real challenge to solve, and I'm most satisfied when the work makes a tangible difference for the people and organizations I partner with.

I work collaboratively and rigorously. I ask good questions. I'm honest when something isn't working. And I stay focused on outcomes, not just deliverables.

How I Work

My Collaboration Approach

I believe the best consulting relationships are built on trust, transparency, and shared commitment to the work. Here's what you can expect from me.

🔍 Strategic First

I don't start writing until the strategy is clear. Every engagement begins with understanding your goals, audiences, and context — so execution is grounded in purpose, not just activity.

💬 Honest Communication

I'll tell you when something isn't working. Constructive honesty — delivered with care — is more valuable than polished agreement. I'm a thinking partner, not just a service provider.

🤝 Genuinely Collaborative

I do my best work alongside engaged clients. I bring the expertise; you bring the organizational knowledge. Together we build something neither of us could produce alone.

✅ Outcome-Oriented

The measure of good work is impact, not effort. I stay focused on what you're actually trying to achieve — and I hold that standard throughout, not just at the final delivery.
