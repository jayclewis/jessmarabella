My Story

From Storyteller to Strategic Partner

Jessica Marabella helps B2B, technology, health, and mission-driven brands turn complex ideas into clearer narratives, stronger content systems, and more credible market-facing communications. Her work sits at the intersection of content strategy, thought leadership, customer storytelling, and PR-aligned reputation content.

Her background includes building content engines from inside growing organizations, leading content and communications teams, managing agency partners, advising executives and SMEs, and developing campaign systems that connect brand, demand, sales, and reputation. She understands how content has to perform inside real organizations, where messaging, market education, stakeholder alignment, and buyer trust all have to work together.

As a consultant, Jessica is especially effective for companies that need senior-level content strategy and execution support without adding a full-time content leader. She helps organizations clarify what they stand for, shape stronger market narratives, and build the content that creates visibility, authority, and momentum.

Jessica is the co-founder of Clewis Consulting LLC (http://www.clewisconsulting.co/) and operates under the DBA Jessica Marabella Consulting. This site focuses specifically on her public relations, thought leadership, and communications work for brands with complex stories to tell.

How I Work

Collaboration Approach

I believe the best consulting relationships are built on trust, transparency, and shared commitment to the work. Here's what you can expect from me.

Strategy Before Output

Strong content starts with clear positioning, audience understanding, and business goals. I focus on the foundation first, so every asset has a clearer purpose.

Clarity Over Complexity

Technical and nuanced topics need language that earns trust without flattening the substance. I help brands make complex ideas easier to understand while preserving credibility.

Systems That Scale

Content performs better when teams have the right briefs, governance, workflows, and measurement. I build a structure around the work so strategy can become repeatable execution.

Proof Matters

Strong narratives need evidence, customer stories, SME insight, and business relevance. I help connect the story to the proof that buyers, stakeholders, and audiences need to trust it.
