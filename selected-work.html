Content Strategy and Growth

Enterprise Technology Content Strategy and Discoverability Support

SaaS Client
Challenge

A scaling, category-leading enterprise technology company needed to strengthen the performance and clarity of its content across a complex market segment.

The organization had an active content program, established product expertise, and a strong point of view, but needed additional strategic support to turn technical subject matter into clearer, more discoverable, market-facing content.

The work required content that could serve multiple goals at once: strengthen organic visibility, support AI-era discoverability, clarify technical narratives, reinforce executive thought leadership, and provide sales and marketing teams with stronger assets for sophisticated enterprise buyers.

Approach

Strategic execution partner, helping translate technical inputs, SME perspectives, and business priorities into content built for search, AI visibility, and buyer relevance.

  • SEO, AEO, and GEO-informed content optimization
  • Technical blog development and refinement
  • Executive and SME thought leadership support
  • Search-informed topic framing
  • Cross-functional review support with marketing, product, and technical stakeholders
  • Refinement of complex enterprise technology narratives for clarity, credibility, and differentiation
Result

The work contributed to stronger content discoverability across traditional search and emerging AEO/GEO channels. Priority technical articles appeared among the site's top-viewed blog content, supported organic and AI-referral visibility, and helped turn complex SAP modernization topics into clearer, more searchable assets for enterprise audiences.

Thought Leadership and Executive Narrative

Personal Brand, Thought Leadership, and Market Education Content

Professional Services Agency
Challenge

A growing personal branding agency serving authors, speakers, experts, and entrepreneurs needed long-form thought leadership content to support its market position and help educate prospective clients on the value of strategic personal branding.

The company was moving toward a more sophisticated consulting model for high-performing individuals and expert-led businesses.

Its content needed to help prospects understand why personal brand strategy, audience clarity, platform selection, and repeatable content production were essential to building visibility, authority, and business growth.

Approach

Developed a series of educational thought leadership articles to clarify the agency's point of view and support its broader business development goals.

  • Long-form thought leadership articles on personal branding and marketing strategy
  • Educational content explaining the business value of personal brand statements
  • Platform strategy content for entrepreneurs and expert-led brands
  • Content production guidance for building a sustainable personal brand content engine
  • Market-facing articles written for authors, speakers, coaches, consultants, and entrepreneurs
  • Practical frameworks translating brand strategy into clearer messaging, content planning, and audience growth
Result

The content strengthened the agency's thought leadership during a period of business growth and rising demand for personal brand strategy. Page views on the articles rose significantly during the pandemic, when professionals, entrepreneurs, and experts were actively rethinking their careers, visibility, and market positioning. The work contributed to a larger content foundation that helped the agency educate prospects, elevate the perceived value of personal branding, and support a more strategic consulting conversation with higher-value clients.

PR, Reputation and Launch Communications

Author Brand, Book Launch, and Mission-Driven Visibility Support

Global Non-Profit
Challenge

A mission-driven nonprofit and its co-founder were preparing to launch a new personal development book while also expanding the founder's public platform, podcast visibility, and broader organizational reputation.

The work needed to support several connected goals at once: build awareness of the book, strengthen the co-founder's personal brand, increase visibility for the organization's mission, sustain book tour momentum, and position the founder as a credible voice in emotional wellness, conscious leadership, and heart-centered living.

Approach

Supported the launch and visibility strategy through PR-aligned communications, media outreach, author event support, podcast guest development, and reputation-building content.

  • Book launch PR and promotional messaging
  • Author signing and speaking event outreach
  • Media pitch development and earned media support
  • Founder bio, positioning, and personal brand messaging
  • Podcast guest outreach and relationship development
  • Visibility strategy for the founder's book, mission, and platform
  • Messaging support across book PR, nonprofit communications, and thought leadership initiatives
Result

The work contributed to a highly visible launch year for the book, the founder, and the organization. The book launched in March 2025, reached the publisher's number one position out of the gate, briefly hit Amazon bestseller status, and went on to receive multiple award recognitions, including two Silver Awards from The BookFest and wins in four International Impact Book Awards categories. Earned media included a founder feature in Voyage Phoenix, and podcast visibility efforts of well-known guests, including David Meltzer, Hal Elrod, and Dr. Thomas J. Rundel.

Customer Storytelling

GovTech Case Studies and Customer Proof Content

Enterprise PaaS Scale-Up
Challenge

A scaling GovTech SaaS company needed stronger customer stories to help public sector buyers understand how its solutions work in real communities. The organization had satisfied customers and meaningful outcomes, but needed those stories translated into clear, credible, buyer-facing proof.

The work required interviewing customers, identifying the strongest narrative angle, connecting product value to real operational outcomes, and creating stories that could support brand trust, demand generation, and sales conversations.

Approach

Wrote customer case studies and success stories first as a freelance writer and later as part of the in-house content team. The work focused on turning customer interviews, product context, and public sector challenges into narrative-driven proof assets that were clear, human, and useful for sales and marketing.

  • Customer interview preparation and storytelling angles
  • Written case study development
  • Customer success narratives
  • Public sector challenge and outcome framing
  • Product value translation for government audiences
  • Quote development and narrative refinement
  • Sales-aligned proof content for buyer education
Result

The work helped create a stronger library of customer proof for a complex B2B/B2G SaaS company. Case studies provided prospective buyers with clearer examples of how peer organizations used the company's solutions to address operational, communication, civic engagement, and service delivery challenges.
