How We Work Together

Engagement Models

Flexible structures designed to fit your timeline, budget, and the nature of the work.

1

Strategy Sprint

A focused 2–4 week engagement designed to tackle one well-defined challenge — a messaging framework, a launch plan, or a content audit. Fast, high-impact, clearly scoped.

2

Project Engagement

A defined-scope project with clear deliverables and milestones — ideal for launches, campaigns, rebrands, or major content initiatives. Typically 6–12 weeks.

3

Ongoing Advisory

A retainer-based partnership where I serve as a trusted communications advisor — available for strategy, review, writing support, and guidance on an ongoing basis.
